About Us

Social Equality and Development Organisation (SEDO)

Social Equality and Development Organisation was founded by dedicated individuals committed to driving social change. With a focus on gender equality, we aim to empower communities by ensuring their rights and Social Justice.

Our organization strives to create a society where social equality is embraced, and every individual is granted their rightful opportunities. We work relentlessly to uplift marginalized groups and promote inclusive development.

Read More

Our Vision

A While human resources are the most powerful resource in this world. Their growth is incomplete and not at all levels. Especially children and women.

Our Mission

A Protect and empower their rights to eradicate various forms of discrimination in the society and raise awareness for social Equality. Creating equal opportunities on the basis of caste, religion, Gender and language.

Our History

Gender Equality is also a Human Rights

OUR HISTORY

The Social Equality and Development Organisation is dedicated to ensuring that education is compulsory for all children between the ages of 0-18 years. Recognizing the challenges faced by marginalized communities, particularly girls, we aim to bridge the gap where education is not guaranteed. Poverty, family pressures, child labour, and child marriage remain significant barriers, threatening the welfare and future of children. Our vision is to create a society where every child, regardless of gender or background, has access to education, safety, and opportunities. Through our initiatives, we strive to combat inequality and ensure a brighter future for all.
