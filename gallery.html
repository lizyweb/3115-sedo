Social Equality and Development Organisation

Social Equality and Development Organisation envisions a world where every individual, especially women and children, is empowered to realize their potential. Our mission is to eradicate discrimination based on caste, religion, gender, and language by raising awareness and creating equal opportunities, fostering a fair and inclusive society for all.
